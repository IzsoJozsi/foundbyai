Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

AEOCheck is an early-stage product helping independent professionals become visible in AI-generated search results. This page is operated as a waitlist and is not yet a commercial service.

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect your email address and, optionally, your profession. We do not collect names, payment information, or any other personal data at this stage.

Why we collect it

Your email address is used solely to notify you when early access becomes available and to send you relevant product updates. We will not contact you for unrelated purposes.

How we store it

Waitlist submissions are stored securely via Tally.so, a GDPR-compliant form provider. We do not store your data on our own servers at this stage. Tally's privacy policy is available at tally.so/privacy.

How long we keep it

We retain your email address until you request deletion or until the waitlist is closed. If the product does not launch, all collected data will be deleted within 90 days of that decision.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@citd.ai and we will respond within 72 hours.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics at this stage. Tally.so may set functional cookies necessary for the form to operate. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

Data breaches

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify all affected users by email and report to the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at aeocheck@gmail.com.
